Atlas User Agreement

1) What Atlas is

Atlas is a portable cognitive profile you generate from your UTF assessment results. When pasted into an AI chat, it helps the AI calibrate its responses to how you actually think and what your current season needs. It is a self-reflection and decision-support tool, nothing more.

2) What Atlas is not

Atlas is not therapy, not medical or mental health treatment, and not a clinical diagnostic tool. It does not provide professional advice of any kind. The AI responses it enables are not a substitute for professional judgment, whether psychological, medical, legal, financial, or otherwise.

3) Your responsibility

By copying and using your Atlas, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • You are solely responsible for how you interpret, apply, or act on any response an AI provides using this profile.
  • Empower.U does not control what any third-party AI says, how it interprets your profile, or what advice it gives.
  • You will use your own judgment before acting on anything an AI tells you.
  • Empower.U accepts no responsibility for decisions, actions, or outcomes resulting from your use of Atlas with any AI system.

4) Data and privacy

Your Atlas contains a structured summary of your assessment results. It does not include your name, raw answers, or any personally identifying information. You control it entirely. Empower.U does not send it anywhere. You decide what any AI receives.

Be aware that when you paste your Atlas into a third-party AI system (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.), it is governed by that platform's privacy policy, not Empower.U's. Only share it in contexts you trust.

5) Appropriate use

Use Atlas for personal insight, self-reflection, and exploring how you think. Do not use it to make high-stakes decisions without human professional input. Do not share another person's profile without their knowledge and consent.

6) Consent

Checking the agreement box confirms you have read and accepted these terms, and that you take full personal responsibility for how you use Atlas.
