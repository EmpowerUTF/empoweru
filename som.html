This SOM submission is locked on this device after results are shown. To change answers, use Retake SOM and start again from the beginning.

How to answer

Each question has three statements. Pick the one that fits you best in the last few months, not how things usually are or how you want them to be.

  • Choose the best fit, even if the other two feel partly true.
  • Answer based on what you have actually been doing or feeling in real life, not what sounds ideal or what you aspire to.
  • If you are unsure, pick the one that has shown up most often.

Forced choice is intentional. It reduces overthinking and makes the signal cleaner.
