Unified Typing
Framework

UTF separates two things most systems blur together: your stable cognitive structure, and your current seasonal state. Not who you should be. Not a flattering label. A clearer read on how you naturally operate, and what is shaping you right now.

The Architecture

COM: Cognitive Operating Mode

Your deeper cognitive structure. COM measures the stable patterns underneath how you think, decide, prioritize, and process reality. It is the base layer of the system, not a temporary state.

21 types across 9 dimensions + the E axis

SOM: Seasonal Operating Mode

Your current seasonal state. SOM measures where your system is right now: available capacity, directional pull, and what kind of season you are operating inside. It modifies expression. It does not replace COM.

9 modes across a 3×3 grid

Together, COM + SOM = your UTF profile. COM tells you what kind of mind is doing the perceiving and deciding. SOM tells you what condition that mind is operating in right now. Example: a Composer in a Driver season is still a Composer, but the current season makes output more available than usual. That is the difference between structure and state, and it is where UTF becomes useful.

What makes UTF different from MBTI, Big Five, and other systems?

▼

Most systems answer only one question. They tell you what kind of person you are in general, or they describe your current condition, but they do not separate the two cleanly. UTF does.

COM measures stable cognitive structure: the deeper patterns underneath how you think, decide, organize, relate, and process complexity. SOM measures current seasonal state: where your capacity is, what your system is leaning toward, and what kind of pressure or opportunity is active right now.

That distinction matters. A structured person in a depleted season does not look the same as a structured person in a high-capacity season. A socially attuned person in a protected season does not express that the same way they would in an expansive one. UTF is built to keep those layers separate, then show how they interact.

It is also dimensional, not binary. You are not being forced into either/or boxes. Different traits can rise together, conflict with each other, or express differently depending on the season. That is closer to how real people actually work.

The Science Behind It

How does UTF measure cognitive patterns?

▼

Dimensional rather than binary

COM is built from multiple continuous dimensions, not forced either/or traits. The framework measures stable differences in pattern recognition, convergence, integration, structure, social reading, refinement, relational weighting, autonomy, emotional steadiness, and social-energy orientation. Your type emerges from the full configuration, not from one isolated trait.

Structured type formation

The COM layer does not assign labels arbitrarily. Types are produced from dimension patterns, thresholds, and type-level structural rules. That means the language on the page is downstream of the measurement model, not a substitute for it.

Seasonal state as a separate layer

SOM uses forced-choice measurement to capture current capacity and directional pull. It is designed to read the season you are in now, not your permanent identity. This matters because stable structure and current state should not be collapsed into one result.

Why the two layers matter together

The synthesis layer is where UTF becomes more than a type label. It shows how a stable cognitive structure is currently being amplified, constrained, supported, or redirected by the season around it. That is what makes the output more usable than systems that measure only identity or only mood.

Atlas: your cognitive advisor

▼

Atlas is what happens when your UTF profile meets any AI. When you activate Atlas on the synthesis page, it generates an Atlas you paste into any AI chat: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, whatever you use. The AI then knows how you think, what your season needs, and how to talk to you.

It's not a separate app. It's a portable profile that makes any AI smarter about you. The AI adapts its tone, advice, examples, and priorities to match your actual wiring and current state. No generic advice. No one-size-fits-all.

Everything stays private. You paste it yourself. Nothing is sent anywhere automatically. You control what any AI knows about you.
