The framework
Two assessments. One structured profile.
COM maps how you think. SOM maps where you are right now. Together they become your UTF profile: a complete picture you can read, reference, and use.
Step 1 • ~15 min
COM

Cognitive Operating Mode. Your stable foundation: how you think, make decisions, and show up consistently across situations, regardless of what's happening around you.

Step 2 • ~5 min
SOM

Seasonal Operating Mode. Your current state: where your energy and focus actually are right now, not where they usually are. Short and precise.

Step 3
UTF Profile

Your COM and SOM combined into one complete profile. A clear breakdown of how you think, where you are right now, and a portable format you can take into any AI conversation.
