The framework
Two assessments. One structured profile.
COM maps how you think. SOM maps where you are right now. Together they become your UTF profile and generate an Atlas payload you can immediately use with AI after the assessment.
Step 1 • ~15 min
COM

Cognitive Operating Mode. Your stable foundation: how you think, make decisions, and show up consistently across situations, regardless of what's happening around you.

Step 2 • ~5 min
SOM

Seasonal Operating Mode. Your current state: where your energy and focus actually are right now, not where they usually are. Short and precise.

Step 3
UTF Profile

Your COM and SOM combined into one complete profile. You also unlock an Atlas payload: a ready-to-use copy/paste profile for AI chats that makes the result immediately usable after the test.

Why this is different
Not a personality test. An honest map of how you actually work.
Two layers, not one Most tools only tell you who you are. UTF also captures where you are right now. Same person, different season, different needs. Both layers change what is useful to you.
Precise, not generic No binary types. No celebrity archetypes. A detailed picture built from how you actually think and decide, not a box with someone else's label on it.
Structured and portable Your profile generates an Atlas payload you can actually use. Paste it into any AI conversation so the AI starts from how you think and what you need before you say a word.
Written to feel accurate Every description is written around how you actually function, not a verdict on your personality. You should recognize yourself in it, not feel sorted into a box.
